My Personal & Professional Project (PPP)

My PPP focuses on building expertise in Secure Cloud Architecture and DevSecOps, with the goal of becoming a Cloud Security Architect who can design and implement secure, compliant cloud infrastructures for organizations.

This project integrates my technical skills in cybersecurity, cloud computing, and system architecture with my interest in compliance frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001) and risk management. Through this PPP, I aim to:

  • Master advanced cloud security concepts and tools
  • Develop expertise in compliance automation and governance
  • Build a professional network in the cybersecurity and cloud computing industries
  • Gain international experience through potential mobility projects
  • Contribute to the development of secure digital infrastructures

This PPP is aligned with the design thinking approach taught at Stanford University, incorporating empathy with industry needs, ideation of multiple career paths, prototyping through internships and projects, and testing through interviews and professional interactions.
